About

Adithyan is a Slope Manager at Indiahikes. He visited the Himalayas for a trek for the first time as a child. The whole experience had such a profound and lasting effect on him. He realised that trekking was his calling. he has joined Indiahikes because he wants to help people to escape the ordeals of their city lives and experience nature in its truest form. he has completed more than 70 treks and 250 hikes. He has led 2 treks to date. He has completed a solo trek covering 500km in 13 days from Manali to Ladhak, traversing it on foot. This experience remains closest to his heart. Backpacking and motorbiking are the other two facets of his adventurous life. He's also an excellent cook.
